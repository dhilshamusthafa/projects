# Charlotte Johnson: Multi-Channel Mastery

### Strategy: The "Double Tap"
- **Step 1**: Send a personalized LinkedIn invite (no pitch).
- **Step 2**: Once they accept, send a 30-second video (Loom) showing how you can solve a specific problem on their website.
- **Step 3**: If no reply, follow up via email mentioning the video.

### Why it works:
It puts a face to the name and shows you actually did the work before reaching out.
