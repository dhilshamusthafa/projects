# Armand Farrokh: 30 Minutes to President's Club (Podcast)

### The "Cold Call Opener" Strategy
Armand is famous for the "PBO" (Permission-Based Opener) shared on his podcast. 

### Key Tactics:
- **The "Poker" Voice**: When cold calling, keep a low, calm, and monotone voice. If you sound too excited, you sound like a "salesperson."
- **The 30-Second Rule**: Ask for 30 seconds to explain the "Reason for the Call" (RFC). If they say yes, you have earned the right to keep talking.
- **The "Triple Touch"**: Armand’s signature move. A call, an email, and a LinkedIn message all sent within 24 hours to maximize "Pattern Interruption."
