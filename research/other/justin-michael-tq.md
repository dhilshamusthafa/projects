# Justin Michael: The "Spear" & JMM Method

### Core Concept: 
Most B2B emails are too long. A "Spear" is a 1-2 sentence email that is so relevant it’s impossible to ignore.

### The Framework:
- **Visual Trigger**: Mention something specific you saw on their LinkedIn or Website (e.g., "Saw your team is expanding in Bangalore").
- **The Spear**: One short question about a specific pain point.
- **The Goal**: To get a "Yes" or "No" quickly, not to book a 30-minute meeting immediately.
