# Nick Cegelski: Problem-Centric Outreach

### Core Philosophy: "Stop Pitching, Start Solving"
Nick focuses on the idea that prospects don't care about your product's features; they only care about their own problems.

### Key Tactical Framework:
- **The Permission-Based Opener (PBO)**: Instead of diving into a pitch, ask: *"I'm sure I caught you in the middle of something, do you have 30 seconds for me to tell you why I called, and you can tell me if we should keep talking?"*
- **The "No-Oriented" Question**: Instead of asking "Is this a good time?", ask *"Is it a bad time to talk?"* (People find it easier to say "No" than "Yes").
- **The Objection Pivot**: When a prospect says "We already have a solution," Nick suggests asking: *"Most people do. Usually, they use [Competitor]. What's the one thing you wish they did better?"*
