# Jason Bay: The Outbound Squad Framework

### Strategy: "Multi-Threading the Decision Making Unit (DMU)"
Jason teaches that you shouldn't just email one person. You need to map the entire "Squad" inside a company.

### The 3 Key Personas:
1. **The User**: The person who actually feels the pain every day (e.g., an SDR). Focus on "Day-to-day ease."
2. **The Champion**: The person who wants the tool and will fight for it (e.g., a Sales Manager). Focus on "Team Performance."
3. **The Economic Buyer**: The person with the budget (e.g., VP of Sales). Focus on "ROI and Revenue Growth."

### The "Reply-First" Sequence:
Jason’s sequences are 22+ days long. He uses a "Value-First" approach where the first 3 touches provide free advice or insights before ever asking for a meeting.
